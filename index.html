FREQUENTLY ASKED

FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions about escrow transfers, payments, and script audits.

How does the secure escrow transfer work?

To protect both parties, the buyer pays the transaction funds into our secure escrow holding. We notify the seller to safely deliver the script files or credentials. Once the buyer verifies the digital asset and approves release, the funds are instantly released to the seller.

What is the platform fee on Vextro Lyntra?

We charge a transparent flat fee of 5% on completed sales. There are zero listing fees, zero listing setup fees, and zero hidden maintenance costs. Review our detailed calculator panel on the Guide page!

Are downloadable digital assets and scripts verified?

Yes! Every Chrome extension, automation scraper, and SaaS script listed on our shop undergoes automated speed audits and direct, manual security integrity validation before being published.

Can I hire the founder Aftab for custom programming?

Absolutely! Aftab takes specialized custom automation requests, Chrome extensions, and API pipeline configurations. Submit an inquiry through the Contact page or text directly on WhatsApp!
